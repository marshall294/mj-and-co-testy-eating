Our Story

MJ & CO began with one simple mission: to make healthy eating accessible, delicious, and exciting for everyone. What started as a small vision has grown into a fast‑rising brand known for premium yogurt, beautifully layered parfaits, fresh toppings, and a dedication to natural, nourishing ingredients.

Our team believes healthy food should never be boring. That’s why we craft every cup with passion — from selecting rich Greek-style yogurt, to choosing the finest fruits, nuts, granola, and blends that give each serving a refreshing burst of flavor.
